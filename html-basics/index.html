ABOUT

Specialized in low-level systems, automation, and 3D printing, enhancing problem-solving and engineering skills

I’m a mix of self-taught and formally trained software engineer, specializing in low-level systems, automation, and building small tools. I’m also passionate about 3D printing and modeling. Problem-solving excites me, and while I often seek the quickest solution, I always focus on saving time and finding the most effective way to address the issue.

I’ve had the opportunity to lead teams in several university modules, where we achieved great results. I assign tasks to team members based on their strengths and how they approach challenges, ensuring we maximize each person’s skills.

Soon, I’ll be earning my bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from the University of Hertfordshire. This will open up a wider range of job opportunities for me.

Fluent in both English and Ukrainian, I’ve worked and studied in international environments. My strong interpersonal skills enable me to contribute effectively to team success.
